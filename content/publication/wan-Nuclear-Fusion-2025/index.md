  \ models tokamak turbulence is substantial demand for high-quality data. Typically,\
  \ producing data involves simulating complex physical processes, which requires\
  \ extensive computing resources. In this work, we propose a fine tuning-based approach\

  \ constructing proof-of-principle ion temperature gradient using datasets generated\
  \ from two gyrokinetic codes, GKW and GX. GX needs terms resources are much lighter\

  \ outcomes indicate tuning methods can significantly decrease needed simulation-driven\
  \ model. Moreover, presented here has potential facilitate development heavy codes\
